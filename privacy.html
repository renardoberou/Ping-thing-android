The Ping Thing — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-08

The Ping Thing is an Android generative spatial sequencer published by Resonant Systems. This policy describes what the app does and does not collect.

Data collection

The app does not require an account and does not intentionally collect personal data, analytics, advertising identifiers, location, contacts, or payment information.

Network use

The app requests INTERNET permission for bundled-web compatibility and font loading/fallback behavior. It does not intentionally upload user content, presets, telemetry, or analytics to a server.

Local storage

The app may store presets, settings, and app state locally on your device using WebView/local storage. This data stays on the device unless you manually export or share it.

Permissions

  • Internet: compatibility/font loading behavior for the bundled web instrument.
  • Vibration: haptic feedback for performance controls.
  • Notifications / foreground service: background-audio playback controls and notification behavior.

Data sharing

The app does not sell or share personal data. It does not include ads or third-party analytics SDKs.

Contact
